Product

What it includes.

Published on Play Store

Focused tools for urge management and daily support.

EUNOIA includes the Urge Search Engine, What If Lab, Daily Tasks, support system flows, and a custom CMS for admin-side uploads and support operations.

The content pipeline uses KOKORO TTS and NotebookLM for humanlike modules and audiobooks, with curated psychiatric RAG material and established books as the input base.
